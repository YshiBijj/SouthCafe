SPECIALTIES

The List of Specialties

At South Cafe, we're not just about serving coffee; we're about sharing the rich tapestry of Laguna's culture, heritage, and flavors with every cup we brew.

Pagsanjan Rush Iced Coffee: A thrilling, cool, and refreshing coffee adventure.

In the picturesque town of Pagsanjan, Laguna, crafted the Pagsanjan Rush Iced Coffee to capture the spirit of the town's thrilling waterfalls and rich coffee tradition. Infused with locally sourced premium beans, traditional flavors, and a touch of sweetness, the beverage became a sensation, blending adventure with tradition. A refreshing and invigorating tribute to Pagsanjan's natural beauty and heritage, it quickly became a symbol of unity and a must-try for both locals and tourists, offering a thrilling, cool, and refreshing coffee adventure in every sip.

Lakeside Lumalaban Mocha: A robust and determined mocha for champions

In the lakeside town of Los Baños, Laguna, Antonio crafted the Lakeside Lumalaban Mocha. Inspired by local athletes, this robust mocha blended the finest coffee beans with cocoa and a touch of honey. It became a rallying cry for champions, fueling the determination of the community. As the aroma filled South Cafe, the Lakeside Lumalaban Mocha became a symbol of resilience, supporting local sports programs and celebrating the unwavering spirit of Los Baños.

Laguna Love Latte: A creamy and sweet coffee that'll make your heart swoon

In the heart of Laguna, Philippines, at the charming "Sulyap Kape" coffee shop, barista Miguel crafted the Laguna Love Latte. Inspired by the region's romantic landscapes, the latte blended local beans with sweet condensed milk and coconut cream, creating a creamy and sweet masterpiece. A symbol of affection, the Laguna Love Latte became a favorite for locals, fostering connections and warm conversations. Served at Sulyap Kape, this delightful drink embodies the essence of Laguna's love and hospitality, making every sip a heartwarming experience in a cup.

Anilag Amore Moch: A mocha with a touch of love from Anilao and Laguna.

Pila Pampatanggal Antok Latte: A latte that's your antidote against sleepiness.

In the historic town of Pila, Laguna, created the Pila Pampatanggal Antok Latte to combat weariness. Crafted from robust local coffee beans and a touch of cocoa, this latte became a staple for hardworking Pileños, offering a revitalizing escape. Beyond being a pick-me-up, it symbolized the town's vitality and close-knit community spirit. A portion of the latte's proceeds supported local initiatives, contributing to the preservation of Pila's cultural heritage. In each sip, the Pila Pampatanggal Antok Latte celebrated endurance and the rich cultural tapestry of Pila

Laguna Luma-Latté: A classic latte with a Laguna twist.

San Pablo Sili-Kape Surprise: A spicy coffee that's a delightful surprise.

In the heart of San Pablo, Laguna, created the San Pablo Sili-Kape Surprise, a spicy coffee delight capturing the city's vibrant spirit. Infused with locally sourced coffee beans and famous San Pablo chili peppers, this surprising concoction is garnished with cinnamon and coconut milk. Served at South Cafe the coffee shop became a hub for those seeking a bold and unexpected coffee adventure. The Sili-Kape Surprise is not just a drink; it's a symbol of collaboration, linking coffee enthusiasts with the agricultural heritage and lively spirit of San Pablo.

Pakil Pampa-Gising Brew: A wake-up brew that energizes like the Pakil Sinulog dance.

In the town of Pakil, Laguna, created the Pakil Pampa-Gising Brew—a coffee that mirrors the energetic spirit of the Pakil Sinulog dance. Infused with locally sourced beans, cinnamon, and a drizzle of wild honey, this wake-up brew at South Cafe captures the vibrancy of Pakil's cultural heritage. As the aroma fills the air, it becomes more than just a coffee; it's a celebration of tradition and a flavorful tribute to the lively spirit that defines Pakil
